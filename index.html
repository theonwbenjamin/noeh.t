About Me.

I’m Theon Benjamin, a Production designer and Multimedia artist based in Cape Town. I create immersive environments, concept visuals, and digital props that bring stories to life. With a degree in Multimedia Applications Development from CPUT, I specialize in 3D modeling, motion graphics, and visual storytelling. I’m actively honing my skills in Blender, Unreal Engine, and DaVinci Resolve to design cinematic worlds for film, animation, and digital media.

My Expertise

3D Modeling

Creating detailed 3D models of products, set pieces, and environments using software like Blender and Unreal engine.

Assets Design

Designing realistic and cinematic props for films, games, or concept visuals — focusing on textures, scale, and visual storytelling.

Environment Design & Art Direction

Creating moodboards, layouts, and visual environments that define a story’s tone, style, and world-building aesthetics.

UX and UI

Creating and designing UX and UI Designs such as digital graphics, web elements, and websites.
